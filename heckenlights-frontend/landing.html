Take control.

Heckenlights is a christmas light installation controlled using MIDI. Heckenlights allows you to take over control and watch it play. Become your self a program director using your christmas MIDI music.

Heckenlights will start on Dec. 1st 2014 and will be available until Jan. 6th 2015. Since christmas lights are cool only at night Heckenlights will operate daily between 17:00 CET to 22:00 CET and 5:00 CET to 8:00 CET.
